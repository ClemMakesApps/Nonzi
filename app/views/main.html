How can I help?

MultiplyMe is designed to help you challenge your community and multiply your donation. You can give directly, or instead use your gift to motivate others to take action. Pledge your gift with a time limit, and if your friends don’t take action before 3 days, the charity loses your gift (you are refunded). If you get three friends to pledge or donate in three days, your donation goes through and we’ll keep track of your impact. If your friends influence others to give, we’ll keep track of that too. Watch your impact spread and your money grow.
