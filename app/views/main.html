Why should you support us?



Back on My Feet is a national non-profit organization that uses running to help those experiencing homelessness change the way they see themselves so they can make real change in their lives. Our mission is not to create runners within the homeless population, but to use running to create self-sufficiency for our Members (those experiencing homelessness).

Through dedication and hard work, Members earn the opportunity to create a new road for themselves. By building a community of individuals who are committed to changing their lives, Back on My Feet provides a support network for our Members both in the short and long term. We also work within the Austin community to raise awareness and change the perception of homelessness.

Since our launch in January 2013, we have served 202 Members in the greater Austin area. Of those, 100 have found employment, many through our local employment partners. We have also helped 42 find housing and have a goal to assist 15 by the end of 2015. Donations made to Back on My Feet enable us to provide job preparedness workshops, assist with apartment lease deposits, purchase interview clothes for Members, cover race fees, provide running gear, and so much more. Give today and help some of the individuals experiencing homelessness in our city move towards independence!
